Grid

The IANS layout grid uses the Foundation grid. It defaults to a 12 column, nestable grid.

This section shows the basics. Please refer to the Foundation documentation for more advanced features: http://foundation.zurb.com/sites/docs/grid.html

Responsive columns

Each grid cell will occupy 12 cells out of 12 on small devices (thereby taking up all the row width). It will take up 6 on medium devices and 3 on large devices.
